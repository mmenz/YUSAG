Shot Recording Tool

Recording a shot

To record a shot, click on the court at the location where
the shot was taken from. Then select make or miss and
finally the player who took the shot.

Using the data

Click on download to download all the shots recorded by
the tool. The columns are as follows:

  • id: a unique identifer for the shot
  • player: the name of the shot-taker
  • outcome: the result of the shot
  • x: the x-coordinate in pixels
  • y: the y-coordinate in pixels
  • date: the date the shot was recorded
  • type: the type of the shot (0=Game, 1=Practice, 2=Scrimmage, 3=Testing)

Converting shot coordinates

To convert the x-coordinate to feet so that the hoop is at (0,0) use:
f(x) = (x - 400) / (667 / 50)
To convert the y-coordinate to feet so that the hoop is at (0,0) use:
g(y) = (561 - y) / (613 / 47)
